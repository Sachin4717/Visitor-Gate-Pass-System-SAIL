How It Works

From Manual Logs to Smart Access

We replace outdated, inefficient paper logbooks with a streamlined digital process. Our system simplifies the entire visitor journey, from arrival to departure, enhancing both security and convenience.

Step 1: Register Account

Quickly create a secure account as a host or regular visitor to manage all your visits in one place.

Step 2: Request for Visit

Log in to your account and fill in the visit details—whom to meet, purpose, and date—to submit a new request.

Step 3: Getting Approved

The host receives an instant notification and approves the request, which automatically generates a visitor E-Pass.

Step 4: Scan and Entry

Simply present the QR code E-Pass sent to your mobile at the gate for a fast and secure entry.
